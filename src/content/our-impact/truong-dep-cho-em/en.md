## Building brighter futures through education

At Homie D'Lion Group, we believe that education is one of the most meaningful ways to create lasting impact.

Through the **"A Better School for Children"** initiative, we partnered with the **Nuoi Em Project** to help fund the construction of **three new classrooms** for **Co Ma 1 Primary School** in Pha Luong Village, Thuan Chau District, Son La Province.

More than a construction project, these classrooms represent the generosity, trust, and shared commitment of everyone who joined us in making this initiative possible.

We hope these new learning spaces will provide children with a safer, brighter environment where they can continue learning and pursuing their dreams.

Homie D'Lion Group remains committed to supporting meaningful community initiatives and creating positive impact wherever we can.
